Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how clickcrafters ("clickcrafters", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you connect your TikTok account and use our integration with an AI assistant (such as Claude) through the Model Context Protocol (MCP) and the TikTok Developer APIs.

1. Information we access from TikTok

When you authorize clickcrafters, TikTok grants us access to specific data based on the permissions (scopes) you approve. Depending on the scopes you grant, this may include:

DataPurpose
Basic profile information (display name, avatar, open ID, union ID)To identify your account and show you which account is connected.
Your videos and post metadataTo display your content and let you manage or reference it.
Analytics and insights (views, likes, comments, engagement)To provide reporting and answer your questions about performance.
Content you create through the app (videos, captions, schedule details)To publish or schedule content to your own TikTok account at your request.

clickcrafters only accesses the scopes you explicitly approve and only acts on requests you initiate.

2. How we use information

We use the information solely to provide the features you request: authenticating your account, displaying your data, generating analytics, and creating or publishing content you direct us to. We do not use your TikTok data for advertising or profiling, and we do not sell it.

3. How we store and protect information

Access tokens are stored securely and used only to make authorized API calls on your behalf. We retain the minimum data needed to operate the integration. Data transmitted between clickcrafters, TikTok, and the AI assistant is encrypted in transit. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

4. Sharing with third parties

To provide the service, relevant data is passed to the connected AI assistant (for example, Anthropic's Claude via MCP) strictly to fulfill your requests. We do not otherwise share your TikTok data with third parties except where required by law. Your data is also handled by TikTok under TikTok's own privacy policy.

5. Data retention and deletion

You can revoke clickcrafters' access at any time from your TikTok account settings, which stops all future access. You may also request deletion of any data we hold by contacting us at the address below, and we will delete it promptly unless retention is legally required.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children

clickcrafters is not directed to individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests, contact us at anas@maznyadeal.com.
